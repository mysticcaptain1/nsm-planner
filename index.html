About sub-threshold

Sub-threshold = 2.5–3.5 mmol/L lactate. You should hold a short sentence but not a full conversation. Reps feel controlled and repeatable. On hilly terrain, use HR zones instead of pace — the terrain will naturally slow you, and that's fine.
